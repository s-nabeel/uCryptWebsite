About uCrypt

  • Secure

    uCrypt uses a custom 256-bit encryption algorithm to encrypt your password(s) to the highest standard. Passwords encrypted with uCrypt would take 3 novemtrigintillion years to crack*, so you're secured. Additionally, uCrypt encrypts and stores your passwords locally, reducing the chances of being compromised.

    *According to Secuirty.org

  • Seamless Integration

    Remebering an encrypted password can be hard, so uCrypt offers seamless login integration for your favourite apps! uCrypt makes it easy to login into Instgram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and more!




  • Flexibility

    Some apps/websites don't support special characters, making it difficult to create a secure password. uCrypt has optional special character support, making it easy to create a secure password without special characters!
